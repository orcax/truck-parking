G-MATS Tool

An interactive, web-based map tool generated by this research enables users to locate nearby truck stops via address or facility type in New Jersey. Basic information including name, type, address and parking spaces are provided for each truck stop. Truck stop can be viewed in state-wide level or watched in street view individually.
