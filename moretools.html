Other Web-Based Mapping Tools

We explored several existing web-based analyzing tools that can be useful for truck parking and other freight-related topics.

By clicking on the snapshot or header of each item, you will be directed to the actual webpages of these tools.

Philly Freight Finder

PhillyFreightFinder is a dynamic, web-based mapping application that pinpoints freight facilities and freight activity in the Philadelphia-Camden-Trenton region.

In addition, this tool also highlights how the various freight system components intertwine and complement one another.

American Truck Parking

American Truck ParkingTM is under development, dedicated to provide dynamic truck parking space availability information to truckers throughout the United States, including private truck stops, logistics terminals, public rest areas, along with truck fueling locations. Amenities are listed in addition to directions along with parking availability and reservations at select locations.

Truck smart parking services

Truck smart parking service (TSPS) website help users to locate the nearby truck parking facility by entering zip code or address.

This tool records the services and payment in each facility. Besides, it allows truck drivers to update dynamic parking spaces of parking facility, however, this service is only provided within partial Michigan State.

Parking carma

ParkingCarma provides information and access to users about available parking spaces via cell phones, the Internet, and onboard automotive computers. ParkingCarma works by matching parking user's needs with available spaces.

Parking lots and other parking asset owners will sign up with ParkingCarma and share their parking space availability.

Ta petro

TA Petro is a web-based tool to find either TA or Petro gas station chains nation-wide. The users can not only locate the nearby gas station but also find out the latest fuel price.

Truck Master Fuel Finder

This is a comprehensive and powerful truck stop-locating tool. This free tool lists the cheapest diesel fuel prices at truck stops between any two points in the United States, or every truck stop in a 100 mile radius around a specified city. Users can follow the link from the truck stop results to a page with the amenities, map, and other details for the truck stop.

Truck Stop and Services

This site helps users navigate the information from computer-based website or mobile app. By integrating two practical functions including locating truck stop and navigating, this tool can help drivers easily to find a nearby truck stop and pre-planning their trips.

Truck Stop Online

A basic truck stop locating tool which enables users to find truck stop by entering address, road network or amenities.
